Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Mutiara Rini (SMK Mutiara Rini) is a public secondary school located in Taman Mutiara Rini, Skudai, Johor, Malaysia.
The school began operating on 1 January 2001.
The campus includes classrooms, science labs, workshops, computer labs, art and music spaces, a surau, a school cooperative, and a health room.
The school motto is “Cemerlang Gemilang” (Excellence and Glory).
The school participates in academic, cultural, and sports activities, including anti-bullying campaigns and team-building programs.
